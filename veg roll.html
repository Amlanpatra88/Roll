Ingredients

For the filling

  1. 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, beans, corn, etc.), finely chopped
  2. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  3. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  4. 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust according to spice preference)
  5. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. 1 teaspoon garam masala
  8. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  9. Salt to taste
  10. 2 tablespoons oil for cooking

For the roll

  1. Tortillas or flatbreads
  2. 1/4 cup all-purpose flour mixed with water to make a paste (for sealing the rolls)
  3. Oil or ghee for shallow frying

Instructions

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for a minute
  • Add the mixed vegetables and shredded cabbage. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  • Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. The filling is now ready. Remove it from heat and let it cool
  • . Take a tortilla or flatbread and place a spoonful of the vegetable filling along one edge
  • Roll the tortilla tightly over the filling, folding in the sides as you go, to form a neat roll.
  • Roll the tortilla tightly over the filling, folding in the sides as you go, to form a neat roll.
  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat
  • Place the rolls seam-side down in the pan and cook until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  • Remove the rolls from the pan and place them on a paper towel to absorb any excess oil.
  • Serve the vegetable rolls hot with your favorite dipping sauce or chutney
