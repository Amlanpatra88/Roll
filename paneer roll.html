RECIPE :--

Ingredients

For the Paneer Filling

  1. 200g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), crumbled
  2. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  3. 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  4. 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  5. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  8. 1 teaspoon garam masala
  9. Salt to taste
  10. 2 tablespoons oil for cooking
  11. Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

For the Rolls

  • Tortillas or flatbreads
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour mixed with water to make a paste (for sealing the rolls)
  • Oil or ghee for shallow frying

instructions

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  • Add chopped bell peppers and cook for 2-3 minutes
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well
  • Add crumbled paneer and cook for an additional 3-5 minutes until everything is well combined
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and remove from heat. Let it cool
  • Take a tortilla or flatbread and place a generous portion of the paneer filling along one edge
  • Roll the tortilla tightly over the filling, folding in the sides as you go, to form a neat roll
  • Use the all-purpose flour paste to seal the edges of the roll.
  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  • Place the rolls seam-side down in the pan and cook until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  • Remove the rolls from the pan and place them on a paper towel to absorb any excess oil.

!Serve the Paneer Rolls hot with mint chutney or your favorite sauce!

!!!Enjoy your delicious Paneer Rolls! Adjust the spices according to your taste preferences!!!
