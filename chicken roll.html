RECIPE :--

Ingredients

For Marinating Chicken

500g boneless chicken, thinly sliced tablespoon ginger-garlic paste teaspoon red chili powder teaspoon turmeric powder teaspoon cumin powder teaspoon coriander powder Salt to taste tablespoons yogurt tablespoons vegetable oil

For the Roll

Thin flatbreads or tortillas (store-bought or homemade) Sliced vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, onions, bell peppers) Mayonnaise or your favorite sauce for dressing Grated cheese (optional)

Instructions

In a bowl, mix the sliced chicken with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, yogurt, and vegetable oil. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse
Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little oil. Cook the marinated chicken until it's fully cooked and has a nice golden brown color. Set aside.

Prepare the Vegetables

Slice the vegetables into thin strips or rings

Assemble the Chicken Rolls

Warm the flatbreads or tortillas in a pan or microwave for a few seconds. Lay out a flatbread and place a portion of the cooked chicken in the center. Add a layer of sliced vegetables on top of the chicken. Drizzle with mayonnaise or your favorite sauce. Optionally, sprinkle grated cheese over the filling

Roll the Chicken Rolls

Fold the sides of the flatbread over the filling, and then roll it tightly from the bottom to form a secure roll

Serve

Cut the rolls in half diagonally for easier handling. Serve the chicken rolls warm with extra sauce or dressing on the side

!!!Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite herbs, spices, or additional ingredients. Enjoy your homemade chicken rolls!!!
